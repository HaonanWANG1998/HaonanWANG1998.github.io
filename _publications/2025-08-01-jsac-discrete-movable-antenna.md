This work establishes that the discrete movable-antenna (MA) positioning problem
is monotone submodular subject to a 2-system constraint, and proposes the DCSPS
algorithm with a provable 1/3-approximation guarantee. A robust extension is
developed for imperfect-CSI scenarios.
