I am **Haonan Wang** (王浩楠), a Ph.D. candidate in Electrical Engineering at the
[City University of Hong Kong](https://www.cityu.edu.hk/), advised by
[Prof. Xianghao Yu](https://www.ee.cityu.edu.hk/~xianghyu/). I am also a Graduate
Student Member of the IEEE. Before joining CityU, I received my M.Eng. in
Information and Communications Engineering (2023) and B.Eng. in Information
Engineering (2020) from [Xi'an Jiaotong University](https://en.xjtu.edu.cn/),
where I worked with Prof. Ang Li.

My research lies at the intersection of **wireless communications**, **signal
processing**, and **mathematical optimization**. I design optimization
frameworks and algorithms that unlock the potential of next-generation antenna
architectures for 6G and beyond.

## Research Interests

- **Reconfigurable & emerging antennas**: reconfigurable MIMO, movable (fluid) antennas, hybrid beamforming
- **Signal processing**: symbol-level precoding, interference exploitation
- **Optimization**: convex optimization, manifold optimization, submodular optimization, matrix analysis
- **Learning**: reinforcement learning for wireless systems

## Selected Highlights

- First-author work spanning *IEEE JSAC* and *IEEE Transactions on Wireless
  Communications*, plus conference papers at ICC, VTC, SAM, and ISWCS.
- Proved that **discrete movable-antenna positioning** is a monotone submodular
  problem and designed a low-complexity algorithm with a provable
  1/3-approximation guarantee.
- **Session Chair** and **TPC Member** for IEEE ICC 2026 (Glasgow, UK), and a
  three-time **Exemplary Reviewer** of *IEEE Communications Letters*.
- Recipient of the **China Scholarship Council (CSC) Full Ph.D. Scholarship**.
