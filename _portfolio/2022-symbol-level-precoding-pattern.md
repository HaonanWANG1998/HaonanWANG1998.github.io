**Xi'an Jiaotong University** &middot; Advisor: Prof. Ang Li &middot; 2022 -- 2023

Reconfigurable antennas add a new degree of freedom—the radiation pattern—that
can be co-designed with the transmit precoder. This project exploits that
synergy at the symbol level.

**Contributions**

- Formulated the joint design of **symbol-level precoding** and reconfigurable
  radiation pattern.
- Proposed an **alternating-optimization** scheme to design the precoder and the
  pattern jointly.
- Developed a **low-complexity** scheme for practical applications and discussed
  antenna-design issues.
