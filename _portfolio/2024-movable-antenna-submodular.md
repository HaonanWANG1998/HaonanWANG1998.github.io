**City University of Hong Kong** &middot; Advisor: Prof. Xianghao Yu &middot; 2024 -- 2025

Movable (fluid) antennas can reshape the wireless channel by repositioning
radiating elements, but selecting positions from a discrete grid is a hard
combinatorial problem. This project develops a principled optimization
foundation for it.

**Contributions**

- Proved that the discrete movable-antenna (MA) positioning problem is
  **monotone submodular** subject to a 2-system constraint.
- Proposed the **DCSPS** algorithm with a theoretical **1/3-approximation**
  guarantee relative to the optimum.
- Extended the framework to **imperfect-CSI** scenarios via a robust DCSPS
  scheme.
